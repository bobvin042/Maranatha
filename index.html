Our Leadership

Dedicated Spiritual Leaders

Our leadership team consists of dedicated pastors and elders who are committed to serving God and our community with wisdom and compassion.

Each member of our leadership team has undergone extensive theological training and brings years of experience in ministry to guide our congregation.

We are committed to biblical teaching, spiritual growth, and serving the needs of our community in the name of Jesus Christ.
